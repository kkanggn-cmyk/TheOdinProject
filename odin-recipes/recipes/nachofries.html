Nacho Fries

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 pound frozen fast food-style fries, such as Ore Ida®

Sauce

  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 2 dashes hot sauce, such as Tapatio®

Directions

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 5 minutes. Stir paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt together in a small bowl; set aside.

  2. Add fries to the air fryer basket, and fry for 5 minutes. Shake, then fry for 5 more minutes. Shake out into a large bowl. Season to taste with paprika mixture. Toss to combine and let rest while you make the sauce.

  3. Tear cheese into small pieces and put in a microwave-safe bowl. Add milk and hot sauce. Microwave on Melt (Low) until just melted; stir to combine. Serve on the side with fries.
